Yanxing: The Rekindling of Han

At the turning point between Wei and Jin, the world is thick with dust and rumor, and heroes fall one by one. Ji Kang, leader of the “Seven Sages of the Bamboo Grove,” is executed. His young daughter, disgusted with the age, flees Luoyang and drifts into the collapsing Shu-Han, where she is swept into war, meets the last idealists of the era, and searches for what “revival” can still mean.

Historical Allegory · Political Philosophy · Strategy & War
